Our Story

Founded in 2010, Stephen Wedding Events has been crafting unforgettable wedding experiences for over a decade. What began as a small floral shop has blossomed into a premier wedding decoration company serving clients worldwide.

Our team of 25 passionate designers, florists, and event planners work tirelessly to bring your vision to life. We believe every love story deserves a beautiful setting, and we're honored to be part of your special day.
